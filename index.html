Who We Are

A Fraternal Family United in Service

The Order of the Eastern Star is the largest fraternal organization in the world open to both men and women, built on the foundations of the Masonic family. Our purpose is to provide a spiritual and social environment where good people are inspired to be better.

Shared Principles
We teach moral lessons based on Biblical figures, promoting essential virtues like Fidelity, Constancy, Loyalty, Faith, and Love through a system of degrees.
Local Fraternal Bond
Leander Chapter No. 73 provides a caring and supportive environment for members and their families in the Leander and Central Texas area.

The Five-Pointed Star

Lessons of the Five Heroines

Each point of our Star symbolizes a virtue, a color, and a heroic woman from Biblical history who displayed great devotion, courage, and faith.

Adah Fidelity

The first point represents **Jephthah's Daughter**, symbolizing loyalty and obedience. Her story teaches us the value of adhering to one's word and moral integrity, regardless of the personal cost.

Ruth Constancy

The yellow point honors **Ruth**, the faithful widow, who embodies steadfast friendship and devotion. She teaches us selfless commitment and perseverance through difficult circumstances.

Esther Loyalty

The white point is dedicated to **Queen Esther**, representing loyalty and courage. She demonstrated moral fortitude by risking her own life to save her people, standing for righteousness and truth.

Martha Faith

The green point represents **Martha**, the sister of Lazarus, symbolizing faith in immortality. Her lesson encourages a steadfast and unwavering belief in divine promises and eternal life.

Electa Love

The red point honors **Electa**, the "elect lady," signifying fortitude and endurance. She teaches us the highest form of Christian love and patience in the face of tribulation and persecution.

Community Outreach

Making a Difference in Leander & Texas

Leander Chapter No. 73 is actively involved in philanthropic work that supports local needs and statewide initiatives through the Grand Chapter of Texas. Our commitment is to uplift the lives of those in need, especially the youth and the elderly.

Patriot Paws Service Dogs

We proudly support the Texas Grand Chapter's endorsement of Patriot Paws, an organization that trains and provides service dogs to disabled American veterans.

ESTARL Scholarships

We raise funds for the Eastern Star Training Awards for Religious Leadership (ESTARL) scholarship program, assisting students pursuing religious vocations.

Youth Support (Rainbow & DeMolay)

We are dedicated to nurturing future leaders by supporting the International Order of the Rainbow for Girls and the Order of DeMolay.
